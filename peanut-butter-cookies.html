Peanut Butter Deliciousness

Description

This dish is something your grandma used to make you when you came over.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup white suger
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Beat peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a bowl until smooth. Stir egg, vanilla extract, and baking soda into the peanut butter mix; stir in chocolate chips while you're at.
  3. Drop mixture by small rounded spoonfuls onto a baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are flattened and golden, about 8 minutes.
