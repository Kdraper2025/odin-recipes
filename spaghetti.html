Spaghetti

Description

This dish is an easy to make spaghetti meal.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams Del Monte Spaghetti Pasta
  • 1 carrot (Gajjar), thinly sliced
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (capscium), thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon Dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes, optional
  • 1/4 cup Fresh cream
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti pasta in hot water and add salt. Take out when the pasta feels good to you.
  2. Strain the water and rinse the pasta with cold water. Drizzle the pasta with olive oil.
  3. Get your tomatoes and cut them in half then add them to your pressure cooker and take out after one whistle.
  4. Once that is done make a puree of the tomatoes after you skin them.
  5. Put oil in a saucepan over medium heat then add garlic and onions. Sautte the onions till tender.
  6. Add all your vegetables to the pan until soft then add the puree, basil leaves, chilli flakes,oregano,salt and pepper, and boil for 3 to 4 minutes.
  7. Add the pasta then stir your cream on high heat until the spaghetti is coated in the sauce.
