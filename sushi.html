Sushi

Description

This is a simple dish on making the best homemade "custom" sushi dish.

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sushi-grade raw salmon or your preferred fish.
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1 avocado
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cover your sheet of seaweed with an even layer of rice.
  2. Layer your ingredients how you see fit then roll it up and slice.
